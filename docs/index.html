About This Course

The Power Automate Academy is a comprehensive training program designed by the Center of Excellence for Finance Operations teams. This hands-on workshop series empowers professionals to transform repetitive, time-consuming tasks into intelligent automated workflows.

Practical Focus

Real-world scenarios from Revenue Management & Collection operations, including rebate approvals, monthly reporting, and data validation workflows

Hands-On Learning

Build real automation flows step-by-step with guided exercises, debugging tips, and best practices from experienced automation developers

Progressive Curriculum

From fundamentals to advanced patterns, each session builds on previous knowledge with increasing complexity and real-world applicability

COE Support

Access to templates, cheat sheets, and ongoing support from the Center of Excellence team for your automation journey
